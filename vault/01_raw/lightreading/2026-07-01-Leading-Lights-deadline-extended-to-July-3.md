Phil Harvey , Editor-in-Chief , Light Reading July 1, 2026 1 Min Read Demand has been strong this season, so we&#x27;re giving everyone a bit more time to get their entries across the line. We&#x27;re extending the deadline for the 22nd Annual Leading Lights Awards to Friday, July 3, 2026, at 11:59 PM PST. Mark your calendar. If you&#x27;ve already entered, thank you. Our panel of expert judges is looking forward to your work. If you haven&#x27;t submitted yet, or you have another project you want us to see, now&#x27;s the time. If you&#x27;re stuck on which category fits best, ask us. Those calls can be tough, and our awards team is happy to help you sort it out. Entry tips Tell us a good story. Keep it concise and back up your claims with data. Don&#x27;t just say you have the best network. Show us how you define &quot;best,&quot; what you measured, the choices you made to get there, and how any of it improved things for your customers. For use case-related entries especially, remember that the story of your customer&#x27;s outcome is the one that best justifies your entry. The product features are nice to know, but don&#x27;t dwell on them if they aren&#x27;t related to the customer&#x27;s story. So, please write well, but don&#x27;t stress about length. Based on positive feedback, we have continued to resist adding a word count limit. You can also include up to three external links to support your entry, and one of those can point to a Dropbox or Google Drive folder with extra articles, charts or research. This is the final extension, so please get your entries in by July 3 .
